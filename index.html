Amader Barir Pujo 2026

Experience the spirit of Durga Puja with devotion, culture, tradition, and togetherness. Join us in celebrating Maa Durga through vibrant festivities, cultural programs, dhunuchi dance, bhog distribution, and community bonding.

Sharodiya Durga Puja

Celebrate the divine arrival of Maa Durga with joy, devotion, and cultural heritage. From mesmerizing pandal decorations to soulful prayers and festive gatherings, Amader Bari Pujo brings the community together in the spirit of Shakti and celebration.

Our Celebrations

Experience The Spirit Of
Durga Puja

Cultural Programs

Enjoy mesmerizing cultural evenings featuring dance performances, traditional Bengali music, drama, and community celebrations during the festive days of Durga Puja.

Prasad

Join us for traditional Durga Puja bhog served with love and devotion. Experience togetherness, warmth, and the spirit of Bengali culture with the entire community.

Sacred Puja Rituals

Participate in the divine rituals of Bodhon, Pushpanjali, Sandhi Puja, and Sindoor Khela as we celebrate the arrival of Maa Durga with devotion and spirituality.

Community & Volunteers

Amader Bari Pujo is powered by passionate volunteers and community members who come together every year to preserve traditions and create unforgettable festive memories.
