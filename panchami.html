We are pleased to invite all for Durga Puja 2026 as we stepped into our 3rd year celebration. The lights are being strung, the dhaks are tuning up, the preparations are in full swing. This year, we will bring back the soul of Bangaliana, tradition, culture, and emotion woven into every moment. From 15th October to 20th October, we'll spend joyful days with Maa Durga, sharing stories, laughter and heartfelt connections.
