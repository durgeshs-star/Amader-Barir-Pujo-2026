🙏 Offer Your Anudan (অনুদান) – Support the Spirit of Amader Barir Pujo

Durga Puja is a celebration of devotion, tradition, and togetherness. We warmly invite you to participate in our Pujo by making an Anudan (অনুদান) towards the various items and arrangements that make this sacred festival possible.

Whether you choose to contribute towards flowers, bhog, sarees, chandmala, decorations, or other Puja essentials, your offering becomes a meaningful part of the rituals and celebrations.

🪔 How You Can Participate

Browse our list of Puja items and select an offering that resonates with you. Every contribution, regardless of its value, helps us preserve and celebrate our cherished traditions with devotion and joy.
